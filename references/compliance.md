# Compliance

Process only public webpages and public business contacts. Do not bypass captchas, logins, paywalls, access restrictions, or rate limits. No proxy pools, forged fingerprints, LinkedIn scraping, SMTP probing, or auto connection requests.

On captcha/login/paywall/429/repeated access error: stop that source, record the error, continue unrelated companies.

Default email mode is `draft_only`; keep send adapters separate from search/scan/score/draft.
